
    remove -- Remove installed packages

    @@ remove ?--at DIR? ?--dry-run? ?--is ENTITY? ?NAME ?VERSION ?ARCH???

        Removes all or specified versions of the NAMEd package from
        the installation. The removal can be restricted to a specific
        VERSION of the package, and even a specific ARCHitecture of
        that. It can also be forced to remove all packages in the
        targeted repository (by specifying neither NAME nor VERSION
        nor ARCHitecture).

        The options are standard options, explanations can be had via

            @@ help options
